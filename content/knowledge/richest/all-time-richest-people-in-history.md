Lists of “the richest ever” can be misleading without context. Wealth in the past was often tied to
**state power**, resource control, and systems of taxation rather than liquid assets.

## How historic wealth is estimated

Modern wealth is usually measured in net worth. Historic wealth is often reconstructed from:
- Control of land, trade routes, and taxation
- Output share of the economy
- Political authority over resources

## Why comparisons are hard

Inflation calculators don’t capture the difference between personal assets and imperial control.
Instead, comparisons work best when focusing on **economic share** and **influence**.

## What matters for WORLD FORUM

Our goal isn’t shock-value. It’s understanding how wealth shaped:
- institutions
- innovation
- inequality
- culture
