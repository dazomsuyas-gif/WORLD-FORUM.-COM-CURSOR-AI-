## Hook (0–5s)
If you start YouTube today, you have a bigger advantage than you think: people don’t follow channels — they follow **clarity**.

## Value (5–60s)
- Pick one problem you solve.
- Make 10 videos for one audience.
- Improve one thing every upload: audio, thumbnail, pacing.

## CTA (last 5s)
Comment “START” and I’ll give you a 10‑video plan.
